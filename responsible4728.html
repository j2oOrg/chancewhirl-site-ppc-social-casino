Signs of Problem Gaming

Be aware of these warning signs that may indicate problematic gaming behavior:

  • Spending excessive time gaming at the expense of other activities
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities
  • Gaming to escape problems or relieve negative feelings
  • Lying to others about the amount of time spent gaming
  • Feeling restless or irritable when unable to game
  • Unsuccessful attempts to control or reduce gaming time
  • Continuing to game despite negative consequences
  • Loss of interest in other hobbies or activities

Tips for Responsible Gaming

  • Set Time Limits: Decide in advance how much time you'll spend gaming and stick to it
  • Take Regular Breaks: Step away from the screen regularly to rest your eyes and mind
  • Balance Your Life: Ensure gaming doesn't interfere with work, relationships, or other important activities
  • Play for Fun: Remember that gaming is entertainment, not a way to solve problems
  • Stay Aware: Monitor your gaming habits and be honest with yourself about any concerns
  • Seek Support: Talk to friends, family, or professionals if you're worried about your gaming
